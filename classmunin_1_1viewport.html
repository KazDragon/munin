◆ get_anchor_bounds()

terminalpp::rectangle munin::viewport::get_anchor_bounds ( ) const

Returns the anchor bounds of the viewport.

The anchor bounds are defined as the bounds in which the top-left corner of the viewport could possibly be in relation to the tracked component. For example, if the underlying component is (20,20), and the viewport was (5,5), then the bounds could by from (0,0)->(15,15) before running out of space. In the rectangle returned, the origin refers to the actual anchor position, and the size refers to the extent of the bounds as described above.
